Welcome to End Hunger Foundation

At End Hunger, we are dedicated to combating hunger and food insecurity around the world. With your support, we can make a difference and create a world where no one goes to bed hungry.

Understanding Hunger: Statistics and Facts

Did you know that over 820 million people worldwide suffer from chronic hunger? Learn more about the scale of the problem and its impact on communities.

In 2019, nearly 9% of the global population was undernourished.

Hunger and malnutrition are the leading risk factors for death and disability worldwide.

Children are particularly vulnerable to the effects of hunger, with millions suffering from stunted growth and developmental delays.

Causes of Hunger

Hunger is often the result of complex factors such as poverty, conflict, and environmental issues. Explore the root causes of hunger and how they contribute to food insecurity.

Poverty

Poverty is a major driver of hunger, with millions of people unable to afford nutritious food.

Wars & Conflicts

Wars and conflicts disrupt food supplies, displacing millions and exacerbating hunger and malnutrition.

Climate Change

Climate change leads to extreme weather events, crop failures, and food shortages, impacting food security worldwide.
